At Generator Media, we are passionate about helping brands unlock their full potential. We believe that truly understanding our client’s consumers – and acting on those insights – is the secret to achieving significant, sustainable growth. We use a variety of research methods and powerful data analysis to identify the best growth opportunities for each client. From there, we build customized holistic strategies designed to achieve measurable results and transformational growth.

We use our proprietary analytics tool Synapse and SynapseAI to analyze data and generate deeper insights than traditional reporting methods. By using predictive analytics, prescriptive analytics and machine learning, we go beyond basic data analysis to forecast future trends, understand complex patterns and make data-driven decisions that propel campaign performance and ROAS.

## Advanced Analytics & AI
Experience the future of marketing analytics with Synapse and SynapseAI. Our proprietary platform combines the power of predictive analytics, prescriptive modeling, and cutting-edge machine learning to transform raw data into actionable intelligence. This sophisticated technology allows us to:
Go beyond surface-level insights: Dive deep into your data to reveal hidden opportunities.
Model complex scenarios: Understand the potential impact of different strategies.
Drive continuous optimization: Adapt and improve your campaigns in real-time.
Synapse and SynapseAI give clients a clear competitive advantage in today's data-driven world
Talk to our team
#### Our Suite of Tools and Services includes:
Synapse: A fully customized Marketing & Media dashboard displaying all your sales, marketing, and media activity, and on-going KPI measurement and calculations – with real-time updates available 24/7.
Synapse+: Real-Time full-funnel Attribution Analytics, customized dynamic multi-touch attribution modeling, and media engagement mapping.
SynapseDiscover: Fully customized and commissioned in-market branded surveying. End-to-end survey management: planning, construction, distribution, collection, and analysis of quantitative and qualitative responses.
SynapseEdge: Near Real-Time Ongoing Competitive and Creative Monitoring across measured media.
Synapse360: It’s your “one point of truth” aggregating all your marketing data in one place, including a sophisticated mapping feature which shows your retail penetration and sales down to the store level.
SynapseBoost: Our Media Mix Modeling solution that evaluates historical data to determine the effectiveness of each media channel in driving consumer behavior and ROI, allowing for the optimization of a media mix for maximum profitability.

# Get To Know Us
For over two decades, our guiding principle has been simple: “We measure our success by that of our clients.” Media is our craft and we possess mastery and certifications across the entire media spectrum spanning from digital to traditional on through to commerce and retail media. Our team is comprised of experienced strategists who create innovative, data-driven strategies designed to achieve a brand’s objectives.

# Get To Know Us
For over two decades, our guiding principle has been simple: “We measure our success by that of our clients.” Media is our craft and we possess mastery and certifications across the entire media spectrum spanning from digital to traditional on through to commerce and retail media. Our team is comprised of experienced strategists who create innovative, data-driven strategies designed to achieve a brand’s objectives.
We've proven that a holistic, full-funnel approach, combining brand building and performance marketing, delivers a significantly higher return on ad spend than performance campaigns alone. And in today's fast-paced world, we prioritize making media instantly actionable – allowing brands to be easily discoverable and/or shoppable.
# How We Work
Generator Media + Analytics is an independent and privately held agency with no ties to any corporate or private equity holding companies. This allows us to invest in client relationships, allocating additional resources and services as needed and enables us to make decisions quickly and in the best interests of our clients, not shareholders, corporate boards, or Wall Street.
At Generator, we understand practical business issues and are skilled at translating business objectives into actionable and measurable communication plans. The agency’s principal partners take an active role in client accounts – working with everyone from account directors to assistant planners – to ensure the highest level of service.
# Our Culture
We promote a highly collaborative culture where every voice is heard and valued. We go the extra mile for our clients, because we understand every interaction, big or small, is important and ultimately leads to creating the most successful campaigns.
