The Client Growth Accelerator®
A system for smarter collaboration, clearer alignment, and scalable
growth.
Built to deliver measurable outcomes and stronger partnerships, the Client Growth Accelerator
helps teams align early, plan better, and optimize continuously, through a four-phase model
grounded in transparency, accountability, and impact:
1. Early Alignment – Define what success looks like with clear goals and shared direction.
2. Shared Goals – Link business outcomes to media objectives that drive results.
3. Operating Model – Clarify roles, responsibilities, and collaboration rhythms.
4. Success Metrics – Establish measurable KPIs to track performance and adjust in real time.

We embed five systems to sustain growth:
1. Learning Agenda (test → learn → apply)
2. Thought Leadership (proactive POVs)
3. Performance Rhythm (bi-weekly + quarterly sessions)
4. Talent Infusion Plan (fresh expertise without disruption)
5. Partnership Feedback Loop (3 touchpoints/year for continuous improvement).
